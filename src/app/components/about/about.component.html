About

Founded by Ram Avadh Kushwaha and initially established as Jay Sharda Construction in the 1990s, The Shree Ram Group (TSRG) has evolved over three decades into a leading national provider of comprehensive multimodal services. With a team exceeding 18,000, TSRG is dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and customer satisfaction across diverse sectors, including industrial works, civil and infrastructure development, engineering solutions, industrial transport, travel, and real estate.

Guided by core values of quality, passion, respect, integrity, and collaboration, TSRG operates under the principle that "Your Vision is Our Mission," ensuring client needs are met with precision and dedication. Prioritizing safety, reliability, and appreciation, TSRG aims to build lasting partnerships, offering a customer-centric approach that consistently surpasses expectations.
